Master Roachi is a work in progress, both the man and the website. I am trying to create a public record of my progress from pretty much no one, to someone who not only builds and creates high quality content but does so in an Orthodox Christian way. This project exists because I do not want my pursuits to remain vague ideas, half-finished attempts, or private ambitions that never become visible work. I want to build things, complete things, study seriously, and leave a clear trail of progress. The three branches of Master Roachi are distinct, but they are not seperate.

Arkitecture

Arkitecture is where I Work Hard. It is the coding branch, focused on web development, skill development, and the long road from beginner to craftsman.

Questicles

Questicles is where I Rest Plenty. It is the gaming branch, focused on livestreamed playthroughs, achievement hunting, and deeper analysis of the games I finish.

The South African Sinner

South African Sinner is where I Study Well. It is the Orthodox apologetics branch, focused on theology, polemics, and serious engagement with South Africans who are willing to ask hard questions about the ancient Christian faith.

The standard across all three branches is the same: do the work, tell the truth, improve over time, and repeat. Everything is public. Nothing is hidden. The weaknesses, the learning process, the unfinished projects, the completed work, and the long-term campaign all belong to the same path. Master Roachi says: Work Hard, Study Well, Rest Plenty Arkitecture Works Hard. South African Sinner Studies Well. Questicles Rests Plenty.
