South African Sinner is the Orthodox apologetics and theology branch of Master Roachi. The channel is focused on helping South Africans, especially Protestants, take Eastern Orthodoxy seriously. The content will include long-form documentary-style videos, Q&A livestreams, and short-form clips. The goal is not to make Orthodoxy trendy. The goal is to ask whether it is true.
