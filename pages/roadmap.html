Master Roachi is a work in progress, both the man and the website. I am trying to create a public record of my progress from pretty much no one, to someone who not only builds and creates high quality content but does so in an Orthodox Christian way. This project exists because I do not want my pursuits to remain vague ideas, half-finished attempts, or private ambitions that never become visible work. I want to build things, complete things, study seriously, and leave a clear trail of progress. The three branches of Master Roachi are distinct, but they are not seperate.

This roadmap tracks the progress of Master Roachi across Arkitecture, Questicles, and South African Sinner.
